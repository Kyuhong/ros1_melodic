

1. Gazebo small house 시작
roslaunch aws_robomaker_small_house_world view_small_house.launch

2. Turtlebot3 Spawn
roslaunch aws_robomaker_small_house_world spawn_turtlebot3.launch

3. 키보드 제어
roslaunch turtlebot3_teleop turtlebot3_teleop_key.launch

또는

rosrun my_second_ros_pkg turtle_move.py


4. Cartographer SLAM 실행

roslaunch turtlebot3_slam turtlebot3_slam.launch  slam_methods:=cartographer configuration_basename:=turtlebot3_lds_2d_gazebo.lua


5. 지도(SLAM Map) 파일 저장

rosrun map_server map_saver -f ~/map



6. 네비게이션 노드 실행
turtlebot3_slam.launch 프로세스 종료 !!!

roslaunch turtlebot3_navigation turtlebot3_navigation.launch map_file:=$HOME/map.yaml
